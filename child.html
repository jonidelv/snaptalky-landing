Child Safety & CSAE Standards - Snaptalky

1. Our Commitment

Snaptalky is committed to ensuring that our platform is never used for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We comply with all applicable child protection laws and standards to keep our community safe.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance stance against child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any activity that exploits or harms children. Any such content or accounts will be removed immediately and reported to the appropriate authorities.

3. Age Restrictions

Snaptalky is designed for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly permit minors to create accounts or use our services. Accounts found to belong to underage users will be terminated.

4. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report harmful or suspicious activity through the in-app reporting feature or by contacting us directly via email at snaptalky@gmail.com. Reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken in every case.

5. Handling of CSAM

Any suspected child sexual abuse material (CSAM) discovered on our platform will be:

  • Immediately removed from our systems
  • Reported to relevant law enforcement authorities
  • Escalated to child protection organizations as required by law

6. Compliance with Laws & Best Practices

We comply with all relevant child protection laws and adopt best practices to safeguard our users and prevent misuse of the platform.
